Table tennis hall


There are 8 ping pong tables in the gymnasium, each of place is 18 meters long, 9 meters wide and 6 meters high. Each court is surrounded by a 75cm high dark barrier, which is separated from the adjacent playing field and spectators. The ground is solid non-slippery dark hardwood. The baffle is light and secure, 1.4 meters wide. The site is located indoors and the wind speed is less than 0.2 m/s.
